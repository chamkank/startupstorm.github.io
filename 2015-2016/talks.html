Who can give a talk

Everyone is welcome to apply and run a workshop! While we can't accept everyone due to limited time slots, we are open to all workshop and talk ideas, from teaching how to build your first NodeJS app, to your first experience with scaling and big data, to learning customer validation as a hacker.

What are the talks about?

Anything cool and hackable! Our talks are geared towards hackers so our topics are usually more engineering heavy. However we aren't also afraid of covering things like customer development, marketeing and getting your first customer as a startup. For an idea of what kind of topics we look for see Hacker News

Where are the workshops held

Most of our workshops are held in Kaiser or MCLD buildings in UBC. However we sometimes do host workshops in company offices DT (e.g. Hootsuite, MarketingAI, etc)

How much does it cost to attend a workshop?

Most of our workshops are 100% free to attend. However for some of our events, we sometimes charge a small entrance fee of 5-10$ to cover food costs or make our events require a membership with our club (which costs 15$ annually).
