| WHO WE ARE

StartupStorm is a club for entrepreneurs, programmers, and designers.

Our mission is to connect students to mentorship, advice, and funding to spring their class projects into multi-million-dollar businesses. We want to ignite the entrepreneurial and building spirit across all 40,000 students of UBC and the Silicon Forest of the Pacific Northwest.

We're taking UBC innovation by storm.
